Core strategic decisions made 2026-04-05 during multi-agent brainstorming session.

**Why:** Astro needed a defensible strategy for Murror as THE app for loneliness, and a plan investors would fund.

**How to apply:** All product decisions should trace back to this strategy. Features should strengthen the emotional memory vault or relationship intelligence graph. Marketing should use "relationship intelligence" positioning, never "therapy" or "mental health treatment."

## Key Decisions

- **Positioning:** "Murror is the reflection partner for young adults who have plenty of people around them but no one who really knows them."
- **Category:** Relationship Intelligence (not "mental health app")
- **Short-term:** Self-awareness + deepen existing connections
- **Long-term:** Community building (new connections)
- **Moat:** Emotional Memory Vault (personal data compounding) + Relationship Intelligence Graph (who matters and how connections evolve)
- **Bold bet (next 6 months):** Emotional Memory Playback — AI references specific past journal entries in conversation
- **Core user:** 18-30, digitally native, emotionally underserved
- **Winning = layered:** Daily habit -> relationship OS -> crisis companion
- **Threats:** Generic AI (ChatGPT) and therapy apps (BetterHelp)
- **Answer to both threats:** Neither has personal emotional history + relationship graph that compounds

## Brand
- Personality: perceptive, unhurried, direct, warm without softening, quietly proud
- One-liner vs ChatGPT: "ChatGPT forgets you the moment you close the tab — Murror has been paying attention to you for months."
- Viral: Emotional Year in Review (Spotify Wrapped for feelings) + Connection Reflection Invite

## Financial Targets
- TAM $17.5B, SAM $2.8B, SOM $6M ARR (Year 2)
- $9.99/mo, 4-6% conversion, $60 LTV, $12-15 CAC, 4:1 LTV:CAC
